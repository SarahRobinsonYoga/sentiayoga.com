Professional yoga instructor in Bath, UK.

Hi, I’m Sarah. I am a certified and registered yoga instructor with the Independent Yoga Network. I’m vailable for studio, corporate and private instruction classes.

In my 23+ years as a student of yoga I have found inspiration, sanctuary and strength in the practice. I try to work one or all of my varied expertise and passions into each class, making each one unique and inspiring! I have created sessions for crossfitters, runners, office workers and complete beginners.

Private Hire

I offer one-to-one classes (£40 for one hour, £50 for 90 minutes). These classes include personalized programmes tailored to individual needs and circumstances. Corporate sessions (from £65) and group sessions (location dependent, ranging from £5 to £30) are also available.

If you have any questions at all, please email me at sarah@sentiayoga.com or reach out to me on Facebook or Twitter. Together we’ll find a way to help you achieve your yoga goals!
